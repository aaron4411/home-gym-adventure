A fun, gamified home gym activity app for kids! Turn exercise time into an exciting adventure with points, levels, badges, and themed activities.

## Features

- **45+ Themed Activities** - Animal and dinosaur-themed exercises like "T-Rex Stomps", "Frog Pond Hop", and "Ninja Warrior Course"
- **Gamification** - Earn XP, level up from Egg to Mythical, and unlock 14 achievement badges
- **Up to 4 Participants** - Each with their own avatar, color theme, and progress tracking
- **Equipment Filtering** - Set your available equipment and activities auto-filter
- **Spinning Wheel** - Random activity selector with fun animation
- **One-Click Completion** - Tap participant buttons to instantly award points
- **Favorites** - Save your favorite activities for quick access
- **Activity Notes** - Add suggestions and variations for future improvements

## Equipment Supported

Saucer cones, foam balls, plyo boxes (6"/12"/18"), Bosu ball, yoga mat, yoga blocks, light weights, balance beam, ballet barre, stepping stones, blankets, Spotify music, and room lights

## How to Use

1. Open the app and go to **Settings**
2. Add participants (name, age, avatar, color)
3. Check off your available equipment
4. Return to the dashboard and start an adventure!
